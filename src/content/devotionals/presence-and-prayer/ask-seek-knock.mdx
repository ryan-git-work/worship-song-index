Jesus says, "Ask, and it will be given to you. Seek, and you will find. Knock, and it will be opened for you."

Prayer is not a performance.
Prayer is dependence.

Worship teams can fall into a pattern where prayer is a transition. A quick moment to check a box before rehearsal. A closing line before soundcheck. A spiritual bow on top of a technical process.

But Jesus is inviting something deeper.

Ask.
Seek.
Knock.

That is active language.

It means you bring real needs to God.
It means you do not stop at surface level.
It means you keep coming, not because you are desperate, but because you trust the Father.

This week, pray like you mean it.

Ask for unity.
Ask for healing in your church.
Ask for courage.
Ask for a new tenderness toward God.

Then keep seeking.

Prayer is not what you do before the work.

Prayer is the work.
