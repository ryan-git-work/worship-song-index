"The Lord's servant must not quarrel."

That is simple. And it is hard.

Worship ministry creates pressure. Opinions. preferences. strong personalities. and it is easy to think leadership means being the loudest voice in the room.

But Scripture ties pastoral authority to gentleness.

Gentleness does not mean you avoid truth.
It means you refuse to fight like the world fights.

Some teams normalize debate as culture. Eye rolls. side comments. power plays. endless critique. That kind of environment does not produce worship. It produces armor.

If you want a healthy team, lead conflict with patience.
Ask questions before you make statements.
Lower your tone before you raise your point.
Correct with gentleness.

You can be clear without being harsh.
You can be firm without being angry.
You can lead without needing to win.
