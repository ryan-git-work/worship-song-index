"He who is faithful in a very little is faithful also in much."

Pastoral leadership is built in small decisions.

Showing up on time.
Communicating clearly.
Keeping your word.
Preparing your part.
Following through.
Owning mistakes quickly.

None of that feels dramatic. But it is formation.

Teams trust leaders who are consistent. Volunteers flourish when expectations are clear and follow through is normal. Churches are served when the platform is not constantly repairing what was avoidable.

Faithfulness is worship.

Not because God is grading you, but because your yes matters.
The little things become the culture.
And the culture becomes the ministry.

This week, do not chase a big breakthrough.
Choose small faithfulness.
